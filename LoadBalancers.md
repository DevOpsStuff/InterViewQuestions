## LoadBalancer

A HAProxy/Nginx LoadBalancers

  **Types of LoadBalancer**
     
     1) DNS RoundRobin
     2) L3/L4 LoadBalancer
     3) L7 LoadBalancer
  
  **Loadbalancer Method**
    
     1) Round Robin(RR)
     2) Weighted RR
     3) Least Loadded (LL)
     4) Least Loaded with slow start
     5) Utilization Limit
     6) Latency
     7) Cascade
